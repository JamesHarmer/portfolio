Search4Local

Website Design & Rebrand

I was tasked with rebranding Search4Local and redesigning the website to enforce brand identity and give the impression of a knowledgeable corporate company that understands how to grow a business through increasing traffic to its website and improving conversion rate.

Project process

  • Step 1: Brand Guidelines

    Develop a clear brand identity and guidelines to enable a clear, consistent design language.

  • Step 2: Wireframe

    Build a wireframe outlining a clear user journey from landing on the site to getting in touch.

  • Step 3: High Fidelity Design

    Convert the wireframe into a high fidelity design using the new brand guidelines.

  • Step 3: Develop

    Build the website in a format that can be easily managed and updated in the future, incluidng additional functionality such as blogs and career application system.
